SUNRISE CAFE

Welcome to Sunrise Cafe. Your home away from home. Sunrise cafe is a charming and cozy eatery known
for it's ambiance delicious breakfast options and stunning view of the morning sun. Nested near the Diani
beach with a history rooted in a passion for good food and community, Sunrise Cafe has become a beloved
spot for locals and travelers seeking a peaceful start to their day, with fresh pastries, artisanal coffee and
hearty breakfast dishes.
